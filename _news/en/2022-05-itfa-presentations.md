I presented two papers at the ITFA (International Trade and Finance Association) Conference. The first paper examined spatial spillovers of interest rates across countries, while the second, co-authored with Jean-Christophe Defraigne, analyzed medium-term cycles in capital flows and their relationship to financial crises in semi-periphery economies.
