Postdoctoral researcher at the [Research Department Transformation Pathways](https://www.pik-potsdam.de/en/institute/departments/transformation-pathways) at the [Potsdam Institute for Climate Impact Research (PIK)](https://www.pik-potsdam.de/en/home) in Germany.
