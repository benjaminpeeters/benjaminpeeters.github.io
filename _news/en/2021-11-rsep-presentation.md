I presented my research on medium-term cycles in capital flows and financial crises at the RSEP (Review of Socio-Economic Perspectives) Conference. The paper, co-authored with Jean-Christophe Defraigne, examines cyclical patterns in international capital flows and their relationship to financial instability in semi-periphery countries.
