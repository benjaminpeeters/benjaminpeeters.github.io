A new working paper titled "Center and Periphery since WWII: history, framework, and estimations" is now available. This paper develops a comprehensive framework for analyzing cyclical patterns in international financial flows between center and semi-peripheral economies, building on the concept of Industrial-Financial cycles of Center Leader economies (CLIF cycles).
