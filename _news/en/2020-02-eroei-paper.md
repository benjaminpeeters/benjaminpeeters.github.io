Our paper "Can the Energy Transition Be Smooth? A General Equilibrium Approach to the EROEI" has been published in _Sustainability_ (MDPI). Co-authored with Jean-Francois Fagnart and Marc Germain, the paper uses a general equilibrium framework to analyze the macroeconomic implications of declining energy return on energy invested (EROEI) during the energy transition.

DOI: [10.3390/su12031176](https://doi.org/10.3390/su12031176)
