Released a new working paper co-authored with Jean-Christophe Defraigne on "Medium-Term Cycles and Financial Crises in Semi-periphery Countries." The paper proposes the concept of Center Leader Industrial-Financial (CLIF) cycles to explain recurrent financial crises in emerging economies.
