About

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Research Department Transformation Pathways at the Potsdam Institute for Climate Impact Research in Germany. I hold a PhD in Economics from UCLouvain (Belgium), where my research focused on empirical assessments of financial interdependencies since the 1970s.

My expertise spans economic history, macroeconomics, climate economics, and international finance.
