## What is RSS?

RSS (Really Simple Syndication) is a web feed format that allows you to subscribe to websites and receive updates in a feed reader of your choice. Popular RSS readers include [Feedly](https://feedly.com), [Inoreader](https://www.inoreader.com), and [NetNewsWire](https://netnewswire.com).
